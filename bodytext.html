body text

Four important considerations

* Meaning, the text that forms the main content of your document.

Though I'll stop short of calling it a rule, I strongly recommend using a serif font - not a sans serif font - for body text.

Most books, newspapers, and magazines use serif fonts for body text. It's the traditional choice and still the best choice.
